# Role Fit Scoring Prompt

Score the role from 0-100 using:

- Role fit: 25
- Domain fit: 20
- Location/work mode fit: 15
- Compensation fit: 15
- Company stage fit: 10
- Background resonance: 10
- Freshness / application likelihood: 5

Return:

1. Total score
2. Category scores
3. Top reasons to apply
4. Risks / concerns
5. Recommended status
6. Recommended resume variant
7. Draft outreach angle
