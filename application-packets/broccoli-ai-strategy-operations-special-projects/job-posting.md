## Role summary

The Strategy & Operations Lead works directly with the CEO on the highest-priority strategic projects across the business. The posting emphasizes this is not a slide-deck role: the person owns workstreams end-to-end, moves between strategy and ground-level execution, and shapes how the company is built.
